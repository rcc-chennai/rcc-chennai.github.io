Making business decisions effectively

For 20 years, Thoughtworks has worked with businesses around the globe - driving innovation faster by developing and delivering more empowering technology that allows retailers to meet their ambitious missions.

Having worked with several leading retail companies , We are now presenting a solution to our local retailers – Retail Command Center, a handy decision support system.

The Retail Command Center focuses on the following

* Yearly Planning 
* * Plan to procure the right product at the right quantity



* Optimize Inventory
* * Optimise stock based on the inventory age
* * Effective utilisation of capital by reducing holding cost



* Manage Suppliers
* * Which suppliers have fast moving goods
* * Are there a few dominant suppliers?

Where does it help me?

If you prefer to not use the automatic generator, push a branch named gh-pages to your repository to create a page manually. Jekyll makes it easy to create site-wide headers and footers without having to copy them across every page. It also offers intelligent blog support and other advanced templating features.
